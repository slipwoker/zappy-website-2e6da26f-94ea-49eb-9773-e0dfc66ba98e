Cold room and freezer shelving – Tonon Italy

Official representation in Israel of the leading global brand. Modular, hygienic and stainless steel shelving systems, which constitute a strict quality standard for industrial storage shelving for factories, retail chains, hotels and the pharmaceutical industry.

Advantages of Tonon systems

❄️

Resistance to extreme temperatures

The shelving is specially adapted for work in deep industrial freezers, and maintains stability and strength even at temperatures down to -30°C.

✨

Maximum hygiene (NSF)

Approved for direct food contact. Perforated and removable polyethylene surfaces, easily washable and sanitizable in industrial dishwashers.

🛡️

100% stainless steel

High-quality anodized aluminum frame that completely prevents corrosion and rust development in humid and wet environments for years.

🔧

Perfect modularity

Quick assembly without screws required. Option to create 90-degree corners and continuous storage for optimal room space utilization.

Leading product series

Heavy Duty series

Heavy-duty storage solutions designed for central cooling warehouses, logistics centers, meat, fish and industrial food plants. Exceptional load capacity per shelf.

Food Service Series

Flexible and compact modular shelving adapted for restaurants, cafes, catering and hotels. Includes removable grids to maintain a clean and compliant work environment.

Pharma & Clean Series

Specially sterile storage systems for pharmaceutical cooling rooms, laboratories, hospitals and clinics, meeting the strictest Ministry of Health standards.
