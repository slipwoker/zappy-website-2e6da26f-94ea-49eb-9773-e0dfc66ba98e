Industrial Storage Solutions Tailored to Every Need

Industrial storage shelves for factories, compactus systems for businesses, industrial furniture for logistics companies and heavy-duty storage solutions.

Industrial Storage Shelves for Factories

We specialize in industrial storage shelving for factories designed to withstand harsh working conditions and exceptionally heavy loads. Our systems are built from high-quality steel with anti-corrosion coating and are adapted to challenging industrial environments.

Every shelving system undergoes rigorous quality testing and is designed by experienced engineers according to the specific needs of your factory. We offer a wide range of heights, widths, and load capacities, starting from 500 kg up to 5 tons per shelf.

  • Heavy-duty capacity up to 5 tons per shelf
  • Anti-corrosion coating for long-lasting durability
  • Full customization to required height and width
  • Professional installation by skilled team
  • Compliance with international safety standards

Heavy-Duty Storage Solutions

Our heavy-duty storage solutions are designed for businesses that store industrial equipment, heavy raw materials, or large products. We provide strong and stable storage systems that ensure maximum safety and exceptional load-bearing capacity.

The systems include special reinforcement rails, reinforced bases, and safety locking mechanisms. Each solution is carefully designed taking into account the equipment weight, access frequency, and the business's logistical needs.

  • Design adapted for extra heavy loads
  • Advanced safety systems
  • Special reinforcements for maximum durability
  • Option for future expansion
  • Professional engineering consultation

Compactus Systems for Businesses

Our business compactus systems offer a smart and space-saving storage solution. Our mobile systems allow savings of up to 50% in storage space, while maintaining full accessibility to all stored items.

The compactus is particularly suitable for archives, equipment warehouses, libraries, laboratories, and logistics centers. The system includes a smooth and quiet sliding mechanism, automatic locking system, and the option to integrate a computerized inventory management system.

  • Save up to 50% in storage space
  • Smooth and quiet sliding mechanism
  • Automatic locking system for safety
  • Option to integrate computerized system
  • Wide variety of sizes and configurations

Industrial Furniture for Logistics Companies

Our industrial furniture for logistics companies includes a wide range of storage and display solutions adapted to the special needs of the logistics industry.
